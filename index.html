Heritage & Vision

I grew up in Yangon, Myanmar, in a conventional family. There was nothing flashy about my upbringing, but I always carried a quiet desire to make something meaningful for the people around me.

Tools like Notion, Streaks, and Duolingo gave me the one thing I had lacked: hope. That’s why I started learning to code — to build small things that lift up people walking in the shoes I used to wear.
